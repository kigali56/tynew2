Chocolate chip muffins

This chocolate chip muffins recipe uses a combination of oil, butter, and buttermilk.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup. semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cups. miniature chocolate chips
  • 2 cups. all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp. baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp. baking soda
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingridients and add chocalate chips
  2. Combine egg,milk and butter and stir into flour mixture.do not over mix
  3. Bake at 370℉ for 20 minutes
